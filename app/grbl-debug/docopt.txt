clinc-grbl-debug

A GRBL debugging utility.

Usage:
  clinc-debug [options] <command>

Options:
  -h --help               Show this screen.
  --version               Show version.
  -b --baudrate=<rate>    Serialport baud rate (default: 115200).
  -s --serialport=<path>  Serialport path (default: '/dev/ttyACM0').
